Pimento Cheese BLT

Ingredients

For the Pimento Cheese

  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnasise
  • 1/2 cup jarred pimentos, drained and chooped
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic poweder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the BLT

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cripsy bacon
  • 1-2 large lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices of ripe tomato
  • 2-3 tablespoons of pimento cheese
