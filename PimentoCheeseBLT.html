Pimento Cheese BLT

Ingredients

For the Pimento Cheese

  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnasise
  • 1/2 cup jarred pimentos, drained and chooped
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic poweder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the BLT

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cripsy bacon
  • 1-2 large lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices of ripe tomato
  • 2-3 tablespoons of pimento cheese

Procedure

Recipe for Pimento Cheese BLT:
Make the Pimento Cheese: In a medium bowl, combine the shredded sharp cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, chopped pimentos, Dijon mustard, lemon juice (or vinegar), garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper if using. Stir everything together until well mixed. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
Prepare the BLT: Toast the bread slices to your preferred level of crispness. Cook the bacon until crispy. Drain on a paper towel to remove excess fat.
Assemble the Pimento Cheese BLT: Spread a generous amount of pimento cheese on one side of each slice of toasted bread. Layer on the lettuce, followed by the tomato slices, and then top with the crispy bacon. Place the other slice of bread on top to complete the sandwich.
Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.
