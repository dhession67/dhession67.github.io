Lemon Poppyseed Pancakes

Ingredients

For the Pancakes

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

    • For the Lemon Syrup

  • 1/4 cup fesh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • A pinch of salt

Procedure

Recipe for Lemon Poppy Seed Pancakes:
Prepare the Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, poppy seeds, and lemon zest. Set aside.
Mix the Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the egg, buttermilk, melted butter, vanilla extract, and fresh lemon juice until fully combined.
Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; it's okay if there are a few lumps. Overmixing can result in tough pancakes.
Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray. Once the pan is hot, pour about 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on one side, or until bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancakes and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through. Repeat with the remaining batter.
Make the Lemon Syrup: While the pancakes are cooking, make the lemon syrup by combining the fresh lemon juice, honey or maple syrup, butter, and a pinch of salt in a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir until the butter is melted and the syrup is smooth. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 1-2 minutes, then remove from heat.
Serve: Stack the pancakes on plates and drizzle with the lemon syrup, or serve with your favorite pancake toppings.
